Rules & Regulations

Before Joining, read the rules and regulations

General Rules

  1. Be respectful and considerate to other members.
  2. Do not engage in any form of harassment or bullying.
  3. Keep discussions on-topic and relevant to the community.
  4. Do not share personal information without consent.
  5. Respect Diversity: Treat all members with respect regardless of their race, gender, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, or background.
  6. No Hate Speech: Do not use offensive language, slurs, or derogatory terms that target individuals or groups.
  7. Stay Civil: Keep conversations civil and avoid personal attacks or aggressive behavior.
  8. Trolling or Harassment: Do not engage in trolling, flaming, or any form of harassment toward other members.
